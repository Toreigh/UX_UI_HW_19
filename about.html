About me.

UX/UI designer with a passion for designing functional, accessible and beautiful products.

More on me.

Hi, I’m Toreigh, an enthusiastic and funny (well at least I think so) UX/UI graduate designer based in Adelaide, Australia. Specialising in empathetic story-telling and creative problem solving I have a great love and respect for the power of team work and collaboration. I’m passionate about designing user-centred solutions that will have a positive impact on people, communities and the environment.

My past life as a special education teacher and disability support worker introduced me to the practice of human-centred design, in addition to empathizing the principles of inspiration, ideation and implementation were key to creating engaging and individualized lessons for each student I worked with. My experience working with a wide range of people has instilled in me the desire to create accessible and inclusive designs, that creates an exceptional user experience.

My Skills.

User Research & Empathy

I begin every project by trying to understand the users needs, motivations and behaviours, initially by making assumptions and then testing them. By observing, questioning and listening deeper insights can be gained and preconceived notions are challenged making room for exciting possibilities.

User Interviews

I use interviews as a part of the user research process, to gain a deeper understanding and empathy for the users needs and motivations. By understanding the goals and aims for any interview I am able to utilise my skills in observation, active listening and empathy to dig deeper.

UI Prototyping

Design and UI prototyping require not only an artistic eye but an understanding of why. I am proficient in creating clickable prototypes in Adobe XD including basic animation into the design as an indication of what the end product could look like.
